Common questions

Is this medical advice?

No. It is advocacy: help understanding your situation and preparing questions and options to discuss with your own care team.

How does the call happen?

Your choice: a Discord voice channel (preferred — you can stream your camera there, so it works like any video meeting), a Zoom call, or a phone call.

When do I get the report?

Within one week of your call, in your case file — downloadable and printable, forever.

Who can see my files?

You and Eric. That's the whole list. Everything is encrypted in transit and at rest.
