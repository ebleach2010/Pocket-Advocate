Service disclaimer & waiver

Please read to the end. In the real app the acknowledge button stays locked until you've scrolled through the whole document.

What this service is

Pocket Advocate is a patient-advocacy service offered to residents of the United States only. Your advocate helps you navigate your medical journey: organizing your story, helping you understand your labs and imaging in plain language, preparing questions to bring to your doctors, and identifying options to discuss with your care team.

What this service is NOT

This service is not medical care. Specifically, it is:

• Not diagnosis, not treatment, and not prescriptions. Nothing said in a discussion, written in chat, or included in a report is a diagnosis, a treatment plan, or medical advice.
• Not a doctor-patient relationship, and not a substitute for one.
• Not therapy and not emergency care. If this is an emergency, call 911.

The advocacy relationship

By purchasing an Advocacy Case you engage the advocate to review the materials you choose to share, discuss them with you, and prepare a written advocacy summary. You remain responsible for all decisions about your health, made together with your own licensed care team.

Limitation of liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, the service's total liability for any claim arising out of an Advocacy Case is limited to the amount you paid for that case.

Privacy & data handling

What we store

Your account email; the files you choose to upload (labs, imaging, records); the recording of your discussion; the written report; and your chat messages.

Where it lives

Google Firebase (United States region), encrypted in transit and at rest. Payments are processed by Stripe — your card details never touch this application.

Who can see it

Exactly two parties: you and your advocate (Eric). If you elect a public session, the live discussion itself is broadcast — your uploaded files and case file are never public.

Retention & deletion

Your case file remains available to you indefinitely after your case closes. You may request deletion of your account and all data at any time, honored within 30 days.

Breach notification

If a breach affecting your data occurs, you will be notified promptly and candidly, consistent with the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule and applicable state law.

Recording consent

Your discussion is recorded

Every Advocacy Case discussion is recorded so the recording can be placed in your private case file for you to revisit, download, and keep.

What you are consenting to

You give written consent for your advocate to record the audio and video of your discussion — Discord, Zoom, or phone. Written consent is collected from every participant before any recording begins, satisfying all-party consent laws (California, Washington, Florida, and others).

Where the recording goes

Into your case file, visible only to you and your advocate — unless you separately and explicitly elect a public session on the next screen. You can revoke a public election any time before the broadcast starts.
