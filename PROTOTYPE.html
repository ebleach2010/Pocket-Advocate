What I can do for you, no fluff

You've sat in the 12-minute appointment. You've googled your own labs at 2am. You've left the specialist's office with more questions than you walked in with. Pocket Advocate exists for exactly that moment: someone in your corner who has lived this, speaks the language, and has the time your doctor doesn't.

The mission

Pocket Advocate is dedicated to empowering patients with neurological conditions throughout Canada and the United States. Founded by a professional patient advocate and autoimmune encephalitis survivor, Eric understands the immense challenges of navigating the medical system. With a deeply personal commitment to client well-being, he offers invaluable support and expertise to help patients navigate their health journey. Pocket Advocate stands firmly on the side of the patient, providing guidance and insight grounded in lived experience to help individuals achieve better health outcomes.

From Eric

I've spoken to hundreds of patients struggling in the medical system, and I have firsthand experience of how debilitating these diseases are, and how disheartening the process can be. My mission statement is simply this: earlier diagnosis and treatment leads to better outcome. My one job is to get you to the finish line faster, so you suffer less and have a better and more robust recovery.

The purpose

Eric is a patient advocate — not a doctor, and that's the point. He doesn't diagnose or treat. He helps you understand what's in front of you: what your labs and imaging actually say in plain English, what questions will get real answers from your care team, and what options are worth raising at your next appointment. Part of the job is locating the best resources for you — including the physicians best suited to your case, chosen with your travel limitations in mind. Serving clients in the US and Canada.

What this service is

Pocket Advocate is a patient-advocacy service offered to residents of the United States and Canada. Your advocate helps you navigate your medical journey: organizing your story, helping you understand your labs and imaging in plain language, preparing questions to bring to your doctors, and identifying options to discuss with your care team.

What this service is NOT

This service is not medical care. It is not diagnosis, not treatment, and not prescriptions. Nothing said in a discussion, written in chat, or included in a report is a diagnosis, a treatment plan, or medical advice. It is not a doctor-patient relationship, and not a substitute for one. It is not therapy and not emergency care — if this is an emergency, call 911.

The advocacy relationship

By purchasing an Advocacy Case you engage the advocate to review the materials you choose to share, discuss them with you, and prepare a written advocacy summary. You remain responsible for all decisions about your health, made together with your own licensed care team.

Limitation of liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, the service's total liability for any claim arising out of an Advocacy Case is limited to the amount you paid for that case.

What we store

Your account email; the files you choose to upload (labs, imaging, records); the recording of your discussion; the written report; and your chat messages.

Where it lives

Google Firebase (United States region), encrypted in transit and at rest. Payments run through Stripe — your card details never touch this application.

Who can see it

Two people: you and Eric. That's the entire list, enforced by security rules on every document and file. If you elect a public session, the live discussion itself is broadcast — your uploads and case file are never public.

Retention & deletion

Your case file stays available to you indefinitely after your case closes. Request deletion of your account and all data any time; honored within 30 days.

Breach notification

If a breach affecting your data occurs, you will be notified promptly and candidly, consistent with the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule and applicable state law.
